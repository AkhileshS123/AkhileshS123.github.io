RRHS Program of Studies

Science

Rock Ridge High School has several advanced classes specializing the four core sciences: environmental science, biology, physics, and chemistry at both the standard and AP levels.

Rock Ridge Science

Technology

CTE classes at Rock Ridge range from technical drawing and design to AP computer science and CIS. Certifications such as the Workplace Readiness Certification and Microsoft Office (MOS) Specialist Certification.

Rock Ridge Technology

Engineering

The Rock Ridge Engineering Program allows for students to engage in an intensive study of engineering while practicing the Engineering Design process. Rock Ride Engineering is inquiry-based with much of the content learned through investigation and problem-based exercises. Students will practice engineering fundamentals, using mathematical and scientific concepts, and they will apply the engineering design process through participation in hands-on engineering projects. Students communicate project-related information through team-based presentations, proposals, and technical reports.

Rock Ridge Engineering

Math

The Rock Ridge Math Department specializes on personal learning, offering courses of various difficulties from basic Algebra 1 to Multivariable Calculus.
