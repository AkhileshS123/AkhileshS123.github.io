Techno Tuners

Started in 2011, the TechnoTuners have been the most successful band ever. But the Tuners have not always been this way. Learn about our story.

Following the success of Coldplay and The Beatles, members of the TechnoTuners became the next group of musicians to restore the concept of the rock band. The TechnoTuners outperformed all of its rivals, selling millions of albums and tracks. After having success in the North America region, the TechnoTuners spread across the world with memorable songs and concerts.

The group was formed by Johnny Tuner, Harry Tuner, Freddy Tuner, and Felix Tuner. At the age of 10, Johnny Tuner, the oldest of the four brothers, developed a passion for rock music. After hearing about the popularity of The Beatles during the 1960s, Johnny Tuner became dedicated to replicate their massive success. He convinced his siblings to engage in musical studies during high school. The brothers worked hard in high school and college to preserve their future. The purpose of this was to epitomize the education of musicians renowned for their exquisite lyrics.

In late 2011, the TechnoTuners released their first album, “Welcome to the Tuners”. After a couple hours, the album exploded and became a hit across the globe. It won multiple awards from several different countries for its diverse lyrics and harmonic audio. The songs “Spiritual” and “Haters” hit number one across the globe. After receiving millions of dollars for producing the album, the TechnoTuners embarked on an international tour in which the band played multiple songs, including “Enlightenment” and “N.O.T.T.I.E”.
